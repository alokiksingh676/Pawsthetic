About Us

Pawsthetic is dedicated to improving the lives of street dogs through feeding, medical care, and finding them loving homes. Pawsthetic is a comunity-driven initiative that aims to provide food, shelter, and medical care to street dogs in need. We believe that every dog deserves a chance at a better life, and we are committed to making that happen.

Our Mission

  • To provide food and medical care to street dogs.
  • To raise awareness about the plight of street dogs.
  • To find loving homes for street dogs in need.
  • Rescue and treat injured dogs.
